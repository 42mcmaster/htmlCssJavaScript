About Mountain View Coffee Co.

Our Story of Community, Quality, and Coffee

Our Story

Mountain View Coffee Co. was founded in 2015 by coffee enthusiast Maria Chen and her college friend David Rodriguez. What started as a small dream to share their love of quality coffee with their community has grown into a beloved local gathering place.

Maria had spent two years traveling through Central America, learning about coffee farming and roasting. David, an experienced barista, had worked at specialty coffee shops across the country. Together, they decided to bring their passion home to Anytown, Ohio.

Today, we serve hundreds of customers each week, from morning commuters to remote workers to families looking for a cozy weekend spot. Our roastery is located just blocks away, where we roast fresh batches of coffee daily.
