## 10. Accessibility Checklist

Plan for accessibility from the start!

- [ ] All images will have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Heading hierarchy will be correct (h1, h2, h3, not skipping levels)
- [ ] Form labels will be properly associated with inputs
- [ ] Color contrast will be high (dark text on light background or vice versa)
- [ ] Navigation will be keyboard-accessible (Tab key works)
- [ ] No flashing or rapid animations that could trigger seizures
