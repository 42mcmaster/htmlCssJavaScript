## Common Questions

### How long is the lesson?
**4-5 class periods** (50 minutes each). Can stretch to 2-3 weeks depending on pace.

### Do students need GitHub accounts?
**Yes.** Free signup at https://github.com/ (takes 2 minutes).

### What do students need to have completed before?
**At least one project from Units 1-4** (for Tasks 13b and DIYTask).

### Can I use just the tasks without the walkthrough?
**Not recommended.** The walkthrough teaches hands-on skills. But solution files have detailed step-by-step instructions.

### What if a student deploys but their site doesn't appear?
See troubleshooting section in [html13c_Task.md](html13c_Task.md) (GitHub takes 1-2 minutes to deploy).

### How do I grade the capstone project?
Use the 25-point rubric in [html13_DIYTask_Solutions.md](html13_DIYTask_Solutions.md).

## Teacher Tips

1. **Demo first:** Deploy a site yourself before teaching it.
2. **Wait for GitHub:** GitHub takes 1-2 minutes to deploy. Have patience and tell students to wait.
3. **Test locally:** Have students test their projects locally (file → open in browser) before deploying.
4. **Use relative paths:** Emphasize relative paths repeatedly ("style.css" not "C:/Users/...").
5. **Celebrate deployments:** Make a big deal out of live websites! Students are excited to see their work on the real internet.
