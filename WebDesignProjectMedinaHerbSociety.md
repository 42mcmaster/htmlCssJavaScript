
# Web Design Challenge: Medina County Herb Society Website

## Project Overview

You’ve been selected to design a **real website** for a **real local client** — the Medina County Herb Society. This is your chance to build something beautiful and functional for a non-profit that supports community education and gardening.

You’ll explore **two approaches to building websites**:

1. A site built using **Wix or WordPress**
2. A second version created using **AI tools** like ChatGPT, Claude, etc.

You’ll research platform costs, make a recommendation, and use AI to help you write, design, and troubleshoot.

---

## Client Background

**Name:** Michelle Sanfilippo, Incoming President
**Organization:** Medina County Herb Society
**Type:** Non-profit volunteer group
**Needs:**

* A clean, informative website
* Herb-themed graphics and event calendar
* Ability to update the site themselves
* Very **low-cost** (or free) options
* Mobile responsive, easy to use

Current site: [Facebook Page](https://www.facebook.com/share/19uG7ijSVg/?mibextid=wwXIfr)

## Phase 2: Site #1 – Build in Wix or WordPress

### Guidelines:

* Use **free version** of your chosen platform
* Keep it **client-friendly** — easy for them to update
* Focus on clean layout, legibility, and organization

### Required Pages:

* **Home** – Intro to the organization with warm, welcoming language
* **About/History** – Mission statement, group history, what they do
* **Events** – Upcoming meeting list or calendar
* **Contact** – Email, social media links, meeting info
* **Photo Gallery** - Download at least 10 from their Facebook photos section 

### Design Notes:

* Use **herb-themed graphics** or colors
* Site must be **mobile-responsive**
* Use consistent font, layout, and color palette - stick with a free template

## Phase 3: Site #2 – Build Using AI Tools

Now recreate the site **with help from AI**.
You can ask AI to incorporate the same functionality as your Wix/Wordpress site or make it slightly different, but the same pages need to be included in your AI version.  

### Required:

* Rebuild the same basic structure/pages
* Use AI to:

  * Generate copy (text) for each page
  * Suggest layouts, color schemes, image ideas
  * Troubleshoot problems
  * Recommend calendar and contact solutions

* You may use tools like:

 * Gemini
 * Copilot
